## Installation Notes

### For iOS Users:

1. Tap the App Store link above or search for "NimoteCode" in the App Store
2. Tap "Get" or the cloud icon to download
3. Open NimoteCode and follow the onboarding guide
4. For existing users can restore purchases from Settings if needed

### For Android Users:

1. Tap the Google Play link above or search for "NimoteCode" in Google Play
2. Tap "Install" to download
3. Open NimoteCode and complete the setup wizard
4. Sign in or create an account to access all features
