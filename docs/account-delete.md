# Account Deletion

**Last Updated:** April 16, 2026

This page explains how to delete your NimoteCode account and associated cloud data.

## In-App Deletion (Recommended)

If you can still sign in:

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Tap **Delete account**.
3. Confirm deletion.

After confirmation, your NimoteCode account and associated cloud user record are deleted.

## Web Deletion Request (When You Cannot Sign In)

If you cannot sign in to the app, send a deletion request through one of these channels:

- Email: aoun.ma@foxmail.com
- GitHub Issues: https://github.com/nimotecode/nimote_issues/issues

Include:

- your account email (if available)
- platform (iOS/Android)
- request type: `account deletion`

## Scope of Deletion

Deletion applies to your NimoteCode account data and associated cloud records used for account/subscription state.

Local workspace files, remote server files, and data stored on your own infrastructure are under your control and are not automatically deleted by account deletion.

## Processing Time

We process verified deletion requests as soon as reasonably possible, subject to legal, security, anti-fraud, and dispute-handling obligations.
